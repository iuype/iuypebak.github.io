Changes in living condition are very obvious。But did want to know the reason behind this ？
What that has changed our lives actually ？

I would like to share my opinions about this topic.

The improvement of living condition can not be achieved without the advancement of technology。From tubes to integrated computers，lots of inventions come up。
In the old year ，the communication between two city is sending a paper letter。But now we can just take a call。

I also found that everything is speeding up.From old days to present days, we move faster and we think faster.So everything is speeding up.

Also ,the iteration of technology is becoming faster and faster. Something new comes up one day and become not fresh at the next day.

I ‘m very positive for this. Though the world is updateing quickly, people can get knowledge from the recurrent process and this knowledge can be stored in the memory。

The only thing left that we have to do is Happy live everyday !
